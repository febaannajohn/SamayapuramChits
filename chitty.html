Frequently Asked Questions on Chits. Everything You Need to Know About Chits

What is Chitty? Who can eligible for chit?
A chit fund is a traditional financial arrangement where a group of individuals come together to contribute fixed amounts periodically into a pool, which is then auctioned off to one member every period. This continues until all members have received the chit amount. The minimum age criteria is 18+ years.
How does a chit fund work?
In a chit fund, members contribute fixed amounts periodically, forming a pool. The pool is then auctioned off, and the highest bidder receives the chit amount. This process repeats until all members have received the chit amount, with the last member receiving it through a reverse auction.
When can a customer participate for Auction?
The customer is eligible for Chit group auction from 2nd month onwards. Before the auction, the Chit member should have paid all his instalments and there should not be any pending instalment in his Chit. If the customer has any pending instalment in his chit, then he will not be eligible to participate in the auction.
